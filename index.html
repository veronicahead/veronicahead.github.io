Hi! I'm Veronica.

I'm an engineer and web developer with a love for problem solving and prototyping.

Welcome, it's nice to meet you.

My fascination for all things cool and unknown began as a kid, exploring the woods and totally acing the science fair each year in school. That naturally led into my career as I pursued Civil, Environmental, and Sustainable Engineering in college and attained a Bachelor and Master Degree at Arizona State University. While in college I was able to get my hands dirty, both physically and digitally. I learned how to prototype and design physical products- ranging from desks to aquaponics systems, to small precision parts - with CAD software and then bring them to life, through rapid prototyping and light manufacturing. I also started to teach myself how to code and learned how to build a website and do data analysis.

I currently work as a Project Engineer at Gayanga Co - a construction engineering tech startup based out of Detroit, MI that specializes in eco-friendly demolition and related public works projects. I am also a 2018 Fellow for Venture for America - a non-profit that aims to create economic opportunity in American cities by mobilizing the next generation of entrepreneurs and equipping them with the skills and resources they need to create jobs. To learn more about my previous work history check out my LinkedIn .
