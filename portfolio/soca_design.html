A Website and Logo for a Cause

A non-profit organization looking to save a historic camp needed a website and brand to support their cause.

The Supporters of Camp Archbald, or SoCA for short, is a non-profit group looking to save a historic scout camp. Their cause was strong and clear, and they had a group of passionate followers all united under the same cause. They began gaining traction and getting press about their fight, but in today's day and age, they needed a website to represent themselves.

I offered to step in and create a website as well as a logo for the group to get their cause out there. I set up a functional site through Wordpress - a free platform - and was able to onboard one of the group's officers as an admin to the site so that they could run it independently. In working with the group I also created several logo mockups for the group. With the site in place, the group was able to run registration for their first annual resident camp this summer.
