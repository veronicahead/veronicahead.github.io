Wordpress Ecommerce Site

Built and designed a Wordpress site with WooCommerce integration.

A Site Fit to Suit

GS Collection is a luxury goods brand that's ready to take their business online.

George Smith is a Detroit based entrepreneur. After getting tired of working for the man and waiting on someone else for his next paycheck, he decided to start his own business. He began sketching custom shoes and soon after began brining them to life. Once he started creating custom shoes, he needed a way to sell them, so he took to Instagram, but quickly found his bandwidth was running out. He needed a website to start selling online.

On this project I worked alongside George and with a team of developers to create a fully integrated ecommerce site on Wordpress with WooCommerce. In doing so we were able to not only create a storefront for GS Collection, but were also able to create content for George's products and integrate his Instagram into other social media platforms. In doing so, our team gave him to tools to bring his business online and streamline his sales.
