Trash Hack Website

A simplistic and functional landing page for a university hackathon.

The RISN Incubator is a university based business incubator at Arizona State University. They focus on accelerating entrepreneurs in the early stages of waste-to-product innovation with the goal of furthering Circular Economy in Phoenix, AZ. As a part of their programming, the incubator hosts an annual hackathon around waste solutions called Trash Hack.

To accomplish this event, the team at RISN Incubator needed a simple website to host information and registration for the hackathon. In taking on this task, I created a single page website for the hackathon with a bootstrap template. The site's content was written with HTML and CSS, and some use of JavaScript. The responsive design can be viewed on desktop and mobile with the same functionality. After several revisions, a final version of the site was reached and the overall hackathon was a success!
